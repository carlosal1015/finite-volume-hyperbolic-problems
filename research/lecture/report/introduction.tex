\section{Introduction}

This work considers a one-dimensional porous medium saturated with
oil and gas ($S_\text{o}+S_\text{g}=1$, where $S_\text{o}$ and
$S_\text{g}$ are oil and gas saturations).
We use the simplified version of the two-phase flow model (gas and
oil) with combustion presented in~\cite{gargar2020combustion}.
\textcolor{red}{
	This work is concerned with heat losses, which we
	consider depending linearly on temperature difference with the
	prevailing temperature, see e.g.~\cite{AKKUTLU2003229}.
}
