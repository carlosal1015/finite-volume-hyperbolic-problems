\section{Numerical results}

In Table \ref{Tabla1}, we used the parameters corresponding to MTO compatible with experimental results in \cite{freitag2005low} for heptane ($C_7H_{16}$) combustion.
This experiment used qualitative testing of the effects of isolating, mixing, and quantitative measurement of reaction rates and product distributions.
Most reactor experiments were conducted in stainless steel tube of $61$ cm long and $1.27$ cm outside diameter with a thermowells placed along the axis for temperature measurements.
The reactor was enclosed in a cylindrical electrical furnace modified to ensure uniform heating.
For tests that focused on reaction rate measurements, the airflow rate was kept high enough to ensure that the oxygen concentration fell only slightly across the reactor in order to obtain an accurate measurement of the partial pressure of oxygen.
